Take Action

Learning about the problem is the first step. Here is what you can actually do to help protect our oceans.

Why it matters

Individual action adds up

3B+ People depending on ocean for food
50% Of our oxygen from the ocean
1 in 6 Jobs globally tied to marine resources
What you can do

Everyday actions that help

01
🧴
Reduce single-use plastic

Say no to plastic bags, straws, and bottles. Switch to reusable alternatives. This directly reduces the 17M+ tons of plastic entering the ocean each year.

02
🏖️
Join a beach cleanup

Beach cleanups remove plastic before it reaches the ocean. Organisations like the Ocean Conservancy run regular events you can join locally.

03
🐟
Choose sustainable seafood

Look for MSC-certified seafood. Avoiding overfished species directly supports the recovery of depleted fish stocks around the world.

04
📢
Sign ocean petitions

Political action starts with public pressure. Sign petitions supporting marine protected areas, plastic bans, and sustainable fishing regulations.

05
💡
Reduce your carbon footprint

Less CO₂ means slower ocean warming and acidification. Take public transport, reduce energy use, and support renewable energy policies.

06
📚
Spread the word

Share what you have learned. Awareness is the foundation of change. The more people understand the problem, the stronger the pressure for action.
