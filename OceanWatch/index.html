About OceanWatch

Why the ocean
needs your attention

The ocean produces 50% of the oxygen we breathe, regulates our climate, and sustains over 3 billion people's livelihoods — yet it is under unprecedented threat from plastic pollution, warming, and overfishing.

OceanWatch translates complex ocean science into accessible, engaging content for students, educators, and anyone who wants to understand and act on these issues.
