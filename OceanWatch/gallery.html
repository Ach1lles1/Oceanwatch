The ocean is both breathtakingly beautiful and under serious threat. These images show both sides of that reality.

Healthy

Vibrant Coral Reef Ecosystem

Coral reefs support 25% of all marine life despite covering less than 1% of the ocean floor.

🐢
Healthy

Sea Turtle in Natural Habitat

Sea turtles have existed for over 100 million years, but all 7 species are now threatened or endangered.

🌡️
Critical

Coral Bleaching Event

When water is too warm, corals expel the algae living in their tissues and turn completely white — a sign of severe stress.

🐋
Threatened

Blue Whale Migration

The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth. Noise pollution and shipping traffic increasingly disrupt their communication and navigation.

🧴
Critical

Ocean Plastic Pollution

17 million metric tons of plastic entered the ocean in 2021. Microplastics are now found even in the deepest ocean trenches.

🌿
Threatened

Kelp Forest

Kelp forests are among the most productive ecosystems on Earth, but warming waters are causing rapid decline in many regions.

⚗️
Critical

Ocean Acidification Impact

Increased CO₂ absorption is making the ocean 26% more acidic since the industrial revolution, dissolving the shells of marine organisms.

🌱
Threatened

Mangrove Coastline

Mangroves protect coastlines, store carbon, and serve as nurseries for countless marine species. 35% have been lost in the last 20 years.
